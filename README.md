This is my first ever factorio mod.
It gives you a recipe to make a bomb made from 5 explosives and 1 electronic circuit. After 10 mins this bomb decays into a 250 damage explosion. Make a bomb and put into your friends inventories :)

Thanks to mod Explosive Spoilage by AVI___ whose code I used for the spoilage explosion.
